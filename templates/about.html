ABOUT US

Our swimming pool booking system company is dedicated to providing a hassle-free booking experience for swimmers of all levels. We partner with safe and well-maintained pools to ensure our customers can swim with peace of mind. Our team is passionate about swimming and committed to providing exceptional customer service.

There are two category i.e ADULT pool which cost Nu.150 and another we have CHILD pool which cost NU.50 per hours.The customer can make reservation for one hour only at a same. If they want to swim more than an hour they have to make multi-reservation.The price of pool are reasonalbe and they also provide swimming tube for those customer who can't swim.
