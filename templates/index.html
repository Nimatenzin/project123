Rules and Regulations for Aqua Blue Swimming Pool


  • No food or drinks allowed in the swimming pool area
  • Slippers and footwear must be removed outside the pool gate
  • Only those with a reserved time slot are permitted to use swimming pool

We would like to remind visitors that the use of the swimming pool is at their own risk. Aqua Blue is not liable for any injuries or accidents that may occur while using the swimming pool area. We ask that all visitors take care to follow the rules and regulations and use the swimming pool area responsibly and with caution. We hope that all visitors have a pleasant and safe experience at Aqua Blue's swimming pool.
